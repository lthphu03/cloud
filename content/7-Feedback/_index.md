During my participation in the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program, I had the opportunity to gain valuable knowledge, improve my technical skills, and experience a professional learning environment. Below are my personal thoughts and feedback based on my internship experience.

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Learning Environment

The learning environment was professional, friendly, and highly supportive. Throughout the program, mentors and community members were willing to answer questions and share their practical experiences. The weekly learning roadmap was well organized, making it easier to follow and complete each milestone.

### 2. Mentor and Community Support

The mentors provided clear explanations and practical guidance whenever I encountered difficulties. Besides the official learning materials, I also received useful advice from other participants through the community channels, which helped me solve problems more efficiently.

### 3. Relevance to My Major

The program matched well with my Information Technology background. Topics such as AWS Cloud, serverless architecture, monitoring, security, and Generative AI complemented what I had learned at university while introducing many modern cloud technologies used in real-world environments.

### 4. Learning Opportunities and Skill Development

Throughout the internship, I improved both my technical and soft skills. I gained hands-on experience with AWS services, Hugo, Markdown, GitHub, documentation writing, and presentation skills. Participating in workshops and community events also broadened my understanding of current cloud and AI trends.

### 5. Community Culture

One of the most impressive aspects of FCAJ was its collaborative culture. Participants were encouraged to share knowledge, discuss solutions, and support one another. This positive environment made learning more enjoyable and motivated me to keep improving.

### 6. Program Organization

The program schedule, assignments, and learning materials were well prepared. Weekly objectives were clearly defined, allowing participants to monitor their own progress. Community events and technical workshops also provided valuable opportunities to connect with industry professionals.

---

## Additional Questions

### What was the most satisfying part of your internship?

The most rewarding experience was being able to apply cloud technologies in practical tasks instead of only learning theory. Building weekly worklogs, attending technical workshops, and interacting with AWS professionals significantly improved my confidence and technical knowledge.

### What could be improved for future participants?

It would be helpful to provide additional hands-on practice sessions or mini projects after each major topic so participants can reinforce what they have learned through real implementation.

### Would you recommend this program to your friends?

Yes. I would definitely recommend FCAJ to students who want to begin their cloud computing journey. The program offers a structured learning path, experienced mentors, practical workshops, and an active learning community that creates an excellent environment for skill development.

---

## Suggestions and Future Expectations

- Continue organizing more technical workshops focusing on real-world cloud solutions and AI applications.
- Include more collaborative mini projects to encourage teamwork and practical experience.
- Expand opportunities for networking with AWS experts and industry professionals.
- I would be happy to participate in future FCAJ activities and advanced learning programs if given the opportunity.

Overall, participating in the **First Cloud AI Journey** program has been a valuable experience. It strengthened my technical foundation, improved my professional skills, and gave me greater confidence in pursuing a career in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence.
