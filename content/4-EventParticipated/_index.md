During the First Cloud AI Journey program and AWS Study Group activities, I had the opportunity to participate in several events related to AI, AWS Cloud, DevOps, data, cybersecurity, and teamwork in the technology field.

These events helped me gain a broader view of how AWS and AI are applied in real-world environments, not only through theory but also through examples, demos, experience sharing, and enterprise use cases. Each event provided useful knowledge that supported my learning process, internship preparation, and future career direction.

During this period, I participated in 3 main events. Each event was a valuable experience that helped me better understand topics such as Generative AI, Amazon CloudFront, Multi-Agent Systems, Docker, GraphRAG, AWS WAF, DevOps Agent, Voice Agent, and AI-powered enterprise productivity.

## Event 1

**Event name:** FCAJ Community Day

**Time:** 08:30 - 12:00 MAY 23 ,2026 

**Location:** Floor 26, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Sai Gon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

**Role in the event:** Participant

**Main content:**
The event focused on AI, AWS Cloud, and real-world technology applications in enterprises. Key topics included the importance of context when using AI, AI Assistant with Amazon Quick, Amazon CloudFront, product development during a hackathon, non-determinism in LLMs, and Multi-Agent Systems for startup credit scoring.

**Value gained:**
After the event, I better understood the role of context in AI, how Amazon CloudFront supports performance and security, and how AI and AWS Cloud can be applied in data analysis, dashboard building, product development, and enterprise decision-making.
